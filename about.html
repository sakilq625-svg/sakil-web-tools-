Why I built Sakil Web Tools

I kept running into the same annoyance — needing to resize an image, merge a couple of PDFs, or knock a file down to under a certain size — and every free tool online wanted me to upload my file to some server, sit through ads, or sign up for an account first. So I built this instead: a small set of everyday utilities that run entirely inside your browser.

Nothing leaves your device

Every tool here — image resizing, PDF merging and splitting, background removal, QR codes — processes your files locally, on your own machine. Nothing gets uploaded anywhere, so there's nothing to store, track, or leak.

What's inside

  • Speed: No server round-trip, so results are near-instant.
  • Privacy by design: Your files never touch a database.
  • No paywalls: Every tool is free, with no forced sign-up.
